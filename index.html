Risk Assessment Matrix

HIGH RISK

Cybersecurity Threats

Increased attack surface with connected devices. Mitigation: Implement robust security protocols, regular updates, and employee training.

HIGH RISK

Skills Gap

Lack of digital skills among workforce. Mitigation: Comprehensive training programs and strategic hiring.

MEDIUM RISK

Integration Complexity

Difficulty connecting legacy systems. Mitigation: Phased approach with professional integration services.

MEDIUM RISK

Data Quality Issues

Inconsistent or poor data quality. Mitigation: Data governance framework and validation processes.
